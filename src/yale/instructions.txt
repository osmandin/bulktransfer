Instructions
------------

Mac
----
1) Mount the share by going to "Connect to Server" via Finder.
2) In the application, click on "Browse Source Folder".
3) Browse to Volumes (typically under "Macintosh HD") and select the relevant directory within the share (typically labelled 'home').
4) Select the target folder.
5) Select overwrite if you want your target files to be replaced.
6) Specify folders of interest in the identifiers box.
7) Once you click "Copy", the Info box and progress bar will update.

On the Mac, sometimes Finder can freeze when connecting a share. Close the application, and unmount the share (through
Finder).
